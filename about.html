My UX/UI journey

Hi, my name is River and I am currently making a career change to become a UX/UI designer because I really enjoy being involved in the process of making something people love to use. I came from a sociology background and have been a freelance photographer which allows me to analyze problems both critically and creatively, and my education background has also given me perspective and passion for social equity which drives me to consider users of all background. In addition, my love for fun and beautiful products also inspires me to incorporate that into my designs. I would love to have a opportunity for a UX/UI role where I get to be a part of a team in making meaningful products for a good cause.
